About About Mineral Trading

Types of Minerals We Offer

Our Animal Feed Minerals Include:

Dicalcium Phosphate (DCP)

A rich source of calcium and phosphorus that promotes strong bones, eggshell formation, and overall growth in poultry and cattle.

Limestone Powder

Natural calcium supplement essential for skeletal strength, eggshell quality, and better feed conversion.

Salt (Feed Grade)

Maintains electrolyte balance, supports digestion, and encourages feed intake in animals.

Magnesium Oxide

Provides magnesium for muscle function and helps reduce stress in poultry and livestock.

Maize fodder

Supports immunity, improves skin and hoof health, and enhances feed efficiency.

Copper Sulphate

Boosts enzyme activity, improves pigmentation, and supports growth performance in birds and animals.
