About About Mineral Trading

We are a trusted supplier of high-purity calcium-based minerals, offering Precipitated Calcium Carbonate, Quicklime, Hydrated Lime, and Limestone for diverse industrial applications. Backed by decades of expertise and modern production systems, we ensure consistency, purity, and performance in every batch. Our facilities operate near rich limestone reserves, enabling efficient manufacturing and large-scale supply across India. With a strong focus on quality control and continuous innovation, we deliver minerals that meet stringent industry standards. Today, we serve a wide network of industries with reliable, customer-centric solutions built on experience, technology, and commitment. Our streamlined logistics ensure timely deliveries to customers nationwide. We continue to expand our capabilities to support growing industrial demands with excellence.
