What is QUICK LIME?

Quicklime, also known as calcium oxide, is a white, solid material made by heating limestone (calcium carbonate) at very high temperatures in modern lime kilns. This heating process removes carbon dioxide and converts limestone into a highly reactive, high-calcium product. Our high-purity, calcium-rich quicklime is produced at temperatures above 1200°C using petroleum coke, ensuring strong and reliable performance. It is widely used in industries such as steel, paper, water and plastic, pH adjustment, acid neutralisation, and pollution control. Quicklime is supplied in the form of powder, lumps, and chips/fines, making it suitable for different industrial needs.

Applications

APPLICATIONS OF QUICKLIME:

Quicklime is widely used across various industries due to its high reactivity and strong alkaline nature. Its ability to improve material quality, control acidity, and remove impurities makes it an essential industrial material

Quicklime for paper industry:

Quicklime helps remove impurities from wood pulp, control pH, and recycle chemicals, ensuring stronger, higher-quality, and cost-effective paper.

Quicklime for Plastic Industry:

Quicklime is used in plastics as a stabilizer and filler, improving strength, heat resistance, and quality while controlling pH and removing impurities.

Quicklime for chemical industries:

Quicklime is used to neutralize acids, control pH, and remove impurities in chemical processes, making reactions safer and more efficient.

Quicklime for sugar industries:

Quicklime is used to purify sugarcane juice by removing impurities and controlling acidity, resulting in cleaner and higher-quality sugar.

Quicklime for water treatment:

Quicklime is used to soften water, remove impurities, and adjust pH levels, ensuring cleaner and safer water for industrial and drinking purposes.
